Freqently Asked Questions

Q: How much of a committment is this club?

A: This club is very important to the members of this club and the leaders take this as a very serious committment. You should expect to put in time for this orginization, and it should be considered a priority of yours.

Q: Am I smart enough for SciOly?

A: The team has a tryoout process, and strongly encourages you to try out, no matter your level of interest, and what you think of your ability, there is a place for everyone on this team!

Q: What are the incentives of Science Olympiad over other clubs?

This is a club where you will not just be able to complete in fun events, where you get to build long lasting relationships with people of all backgrounds, but also one where we are competitive and with the right effort are able to place well, which will look good on a resume, or college application.

Q: How do tryouts for this team work?

A: There will be mandantory tryouts for this team in early summer, where you will take a test for the category that you would like to compete in, ranging from Chemistry, to Biology and Earth Science.

Q: Is Science Olympiad difficult?

A: The events that are dealt out by the overarching committee are usually difficult, with with the right kind of skill, and a good partner, you can master any type of event.
